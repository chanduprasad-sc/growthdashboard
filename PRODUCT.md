# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Marketing operators, founders, content teams, and support teams who need to create short, memorable links, share QR codes, and understand where clicks are coming from without opening a heavyweight analytics suite.

## Product Purpose

MiniLinks turns long URLs into managed short links with custom aliases, campaign labels, QR-ready sharing, redirects, and click analytics. Success means a user can create a link in seconds, trust that redirects are reliable, and inspect performance from the same workspace.

## Brand Personality

Precise, fast, trustworthy. The interface should feel like an operational tool: clear hierarchy, restrained color, strong feedback, and no decorative friction.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS hero clutter, purely static mockups, analytics hidden away from the creation workflow, playful URL-shortener gimmicks, and visual styles that make standard form controls feel unfamiliar.

## Design Principles

Show the shortener and analytics together.
Make link state visible immediately.
Use familiar controls for fast repeated work.
Treat trust, reliability, and tracking as core features.
Keep marketing inspiration in the product surface, not as decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, readable focus states, non-color-only status indicators, and reduced-motion friendly transitions.
